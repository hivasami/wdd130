History

This adventorous company was created on 1990, in this beautiful contry, for the purpose of your fun and extreme experiences for your life. When an curious family decided that their weekend hobby of exploring hidden rivers deserved to become something bigger. What started as a beat‑up inflatable raft, a borrowed truck, and a shared love of the outdoors slowly grew into one of the region’s most beloved adventure companies.
